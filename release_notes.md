# Play Store Release Notes - Offline Media Player (v1.0.0)

## Short Description (80 characters)
Experience your local music and videos with smart analytics and dynamic themes.

## Full Description
Welcome to the first release of Offline Media Player! 

Our goal is to provide a seamless, all-in-one media experience for your local files, combined with personalized insights that help you rediscover your favorite content.

### Key Features:
*   **Universal Media Player:** One app for all your local music, videos, and images.
*   **Personalized Analytics:** Dive into your listening habits with the "Me" screen. Track your daily/weekly playtime, streaks, and all-time favorites.
*   **Continue Watching:** Never lose your place again. Resume videos and audio tracks exactly where you left off.
*   **Advanced Video Playback:**
    *   **Picture-in-Picture (PiP):** Multitask without missing a frame.
    *   **Intuitive Gestures:** Swipe to control volume, brightness, and seeking.
    *   **Track Selection:** Switch between different audio streams and subtitles.
    *   **Resize Modes:** Choose between Fit, Fill, or Zoom for the perfect view.
*   **Smart Library:** 
    *   Automatic organization by Artist and Album.
    *   Custom Playlists for both audio and video files.
    *   Advanced sorting (Title, Date Added, Duration, Most Played).
*   **Dynamic Customization:** Express yourself with Orange, Blue, or Green themes, fully compatible with Light and Dark modes.
*   **Edge-to-Edge Design:** A modern UI that uses every pixel of your screen.
*   **Accessibility First:** Includes a dedicated guide to ensure everyone can enjoy their media.

## What's New (for Play Store Release Notes)
*   **Initial Release!**
*   Full support for local audio and video playback.
*   Personalized usage analytics and "Continue Watching" history.
*   Picture-in-Picture mode for video multitasking.
*   Gesture-based video controls (Volume, Brightness, Seek).
*   Dynamic themes (Orange, Blue, Green) with Dark Mode support.
*   Smart library organization and custom playlists.

## Technical Highlights (Internal/Closed Testing focus)
*   **Modern Architecture:** Built 100% with Jetpack Compose and Hilt.
*   **Robust Playback:** Powered by Google's Media3 (ExoPlayer) library.
*   **Local-First:** Privacy-focused with local Room database for stats and metadata.
*   **Performance:** Optimized with Kotlin Coroutines and StateFlow for a smooth 60fps experience.
